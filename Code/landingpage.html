About Us

This is a free PC parts compatibility checker website for those who want to build an entirely new and customized PC for their needs. We will help you to customize your high-end pc by picking up the budget-friendly components. We will do so by recommending different builds — sets of computers that meet specific goals. This site lets you start plugging in components for your build and make sure everything is compatible before you drop big bucks on hardware. It will also provide the compatibility guide to make sure that the components you are choosing to build your own PC are compatible.
